Seating

Designed for acoustics and comfort

Crescendo Hall offers tiered seating with carefully tuned sightlines so that each level feels connected to the performance. Accessible seating is available and may be arranged directly with guest services.

Purchase Information

How guests typically buy

Most guests choose a performance on the Events page, review the seating tier that fits their evening, and complete purchase online in a single step.

For accessibility needs, group assistance, or questions about a specific event, guest services can help before checkout.
